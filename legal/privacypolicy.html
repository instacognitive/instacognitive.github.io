Instacognitive is a service for Instagram users to analyze the contents of their pictures using Microsoft Cognitive Services. When Instagram users upload photos using the #instacognitive hashtag, this service will analyze their photo and feed back a caption which describes the contents of the photo as a comment on that user's post.

This Privacy Policy describes how and when Instacognitive for Instagram collects, uses and shares your information when you use our Services. Instacognitive solely looks for public Instagram posts using the #instacognitive hashtag. Only for these specific posts, the image URL and Post ID are captured to respectively analyze the photo and post a comment on that photo. Instacognitive for Instagram does not receive or collect any user information. We however do not store this data, and this is only used to make a one-time comment on your photo.

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@instacognitive.com.
